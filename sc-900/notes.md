## Key concepts

### Zero Trust

Zero Trust is based on the idea of never automatically trusting users, devices, or networks. Access should be verified continuously.

### Identity

Identity is central to modern security because users, devices, and applications need controlled access to systems and data.

### Compliance

Compliance helps organizations follow laws, regulations, policies, and security standards.
